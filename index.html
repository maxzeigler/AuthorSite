Hanging from Yggdrasil

Ancient Wisdom for the Technological Age

by Max Zeigler

Hanging from Yggdrasil explores the eternal balance between violence and wisdom, examining how ancient insights from Norse mythology can illuminate our path through modern technological upheaval. Drawing on history, philosophy, and myth, Max Zeigler invites readers to reflect on the profound need for sacrifice, discipline, and aesthetic vision in shaping a world driven by unprecedented technological power.

The book argues that only by reuniting force and foresight, power and wisdom, can society navigate the coming storms of artificial intelligence, genetic engineering, and robotics, and forge something meaningful and lasting from the chaos.

About the Author

Max Zeigler writes about the intersection of history, philosophy, and technology. His insights draw deeply from classical traditions, particularly Norse mythology, to address contemporary challenges.
